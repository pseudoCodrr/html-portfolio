The Best Movies According to Shantanu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Matrix (1999)

Gener: Sci-Fi, Action

This is my favorite sci-fi movie because it truly blows my mind and forces me to think outside the box. At one point, I was even convinced that someone beyond this world might be controlling us!

Chhaava (2025)

Gener: History, Action, Drama

Chhaava (2025) is a grand historical drama that vividly portrays the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, the fearless warrior and son of the legendary Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. With stunning visuals and a gripping narrative, the film pays tribute to his bravery and leadership. It offers an immersive journey into a crucial chapter of Indian history, bringing to life the struggles and triumphs of a remarkable ruler.

Shaadi Mein Zaroor Aana (2017)

Gener: Romance, Comedy, Drama

This is a romantic drama film about a girl and a boy who are brought together by a marriage proposal and fall in love with each other but on the night of their marriage, destiny takes the two of them in two different directions. The movie is a perfect Bollywood film with love songs, romantic U-turns, 2 warring families and a an expected ending with a twist.
